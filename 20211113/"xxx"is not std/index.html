运行slam14_ch3代码visualizeGeometry报错error: ‘xxxx’ was not declared in this scope

亲测方案：CMakeLists.txt 添加 set(CMAKE_CXX_STANDARD 14)
或者：set(CMAKE_CXX_FLAGS “-std=c++14 -O2 ${SSE_FLAGS} -msse4”)
